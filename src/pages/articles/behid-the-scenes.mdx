A Day in the Life of a 17-Year-Old Level 10 Gymnast

The day to day life of an elite gymnast revolves completely around the gym. You typically workout 6-7 hours a day with 1 day off each week. This means that school is fit in around that schedule. For some, they homeschool in order to to have more time in the gym.
